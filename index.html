About

I finished my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park in September 2015. After graduating, I spent 3 years as a research scientist at Intel Labs near Portland, Oregon. Most recently I joined Microsoft in 2018, where I work as a research software engineer. My interests are broad, and generally I enjoy scalability and performance challenges related to systems infrastructure.
